Who we are

We're a bunch of experts with doctorate degrees in education and pychology...yeah right.

We're students just like you.

All the stuff you see here is made by students, for students.

How we help you

What we have here is basically an online platform that seeks to assist students learning by providing detailed yet, simplified lecture notes.

It is a platform which provides students with a myriad of courses beyond a specification. Students can get all the necessary and timely information about most courses offered in University of Ghana.

We don't put slides on the site, No! We deduce understanding from the slides, add lecture notes, and upload it on the damzinium.com site to enhance student learning in a way that suits everybody.
